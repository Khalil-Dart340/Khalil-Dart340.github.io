Sweet Potato Crunch Recipe

Serves 4-6

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups of Sweet Potatoes
  • 2 cups of Sugar
  • 1 cup of butter melted
  • 4 Eggs beaten
  • 2 tablespoons of Vanilla
  • 1 cups of Milk
  • 2 teaspoons of Cinnamon
  • 1 cup of Brown Sugar packed
  • 1/2 cup of Flour
  • 1 cup of Chopped Peacans/Walnuts

How to Make:

  1. Preheat oven to 375*
  2. combine Sweet Potatoes, Sugar, 1/2 cup Butter, Eggs, Vanilla, Milk, and Flour. Mix Well
  3. Put into a 2 quart baking dish
  4. Combine remaining Butter, Cinnamon, Brown Sugar and Pecans together.
  5. Sprinkle over Potatoes
  6. Put in the oven for 45-60 minutes or until the top browns and isn't liquidy.
